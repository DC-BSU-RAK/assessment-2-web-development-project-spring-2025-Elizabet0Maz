About Me

Hello, I’m Elizabet Maznychenko, a passionate painter dedicated to crafting fantastical worlds that operate on their whimsical logic. In my artwork, you may encounter a vintage clock intricately embedded in an ornate teapot, as it pours its liquid gold into a cascading waterfall. You might also discover chess pieces artfully scattered across landscapes overflowing with gentle chaos, inviting viewers to explore the unexpected. My aim is not to depict reality as it is but to evoke a sense of wonder by imagining realms on the fringes of dreams, where vivid colours and deep emotions intertwine. I find inspiration in the delightful interplay between wonder and eccentricity. I am captivated by sunsets that seem almost unreal, their hues too perfect to be natural. Additionally, I draw from objects that evoke the charm of fairy tales, bringing a touch of magic to the mundane. My artistic journey also involves experimenting with digital tools like Photoshop and photography, where I continually seek to uncover that enchanting “what if?” feeling that sparks creativity. Through my art, I aim to create a portal that transports you to another universe and a unique perspective on life itself. I am truly grateful for your willingness to step into this imaginative space with me.
